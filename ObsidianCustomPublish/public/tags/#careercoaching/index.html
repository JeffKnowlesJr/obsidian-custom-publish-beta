    202501311633 Meeting Notes

    January 31, 2025
    #CareerCoaching

    Overview #

    In the recent One-on-One Coaching Session, Ben and Jeff focused on Jeff’s current projects, including challenges with the GMU application and freelance client SLAs. They discussed client relationships, billing consistency, and Jeff’s desire to enhance his UX/UI design skills. The session emphasized strategic content mapping and communication improvements. Ben introduced coaching strategies, and they agreed on a monthly coaching plan with specific action items for Jeff.

    Detailed Notes #

    Current Projects and Challenges (00:01 - 18:35) #

    • Jeff is working on three web projects, a school application, and a personal website.
    • There is uncertainty about the transfer student status for the GMU application.
    • Jeff is implementing a new SLA for freelance clients and mapping operations.
    • Concerns exist about service quality and process gaps.

    Client Relationships and Billing (18:35 - 31:40) #

    • Discussion on managing multiple client projects and websites.
    • Implementation of Halo PSA for billing, facing challenges with inconsistent billing.
    • Jeff desires more knowledge in UX/UI design and content development.

    Content Strategy and Planning (31:40 - 40:39) #

    • Lack of strategy for content mapping and understanding of UI/UX flow.
    • Interest in developing a personal website as a learning experience.

    Process Improvement and Self-Reflection (40:39 - 55:20) #

    • Importance of planning before execution and communicating decisions to clients.
    • Need for better project planning and communication.

    Coaching Strategies and Future Plans (55:22 - 01:02:26) #

    • Introduction of ‘what’ questions technique for better understanding.
    • Proposal for monthly coaching sessions.

    Wrap-up and Next Steps (01:02:26 - 01:04:22) #

    • Agreement on a monthly coaching plan and scheduling the next session for the end of February.

    Action Items for Jeff #

    • Create a content deck for ongoing projects.
    • Start planning a personal website using a structured process.
    • Implement ‘what’ questioning technique with clients.
    • Schedule the next coaching session for the end of February.

    Resources and Recommendations #

    Web Development & UX/UI Design #

    1. Refactoring UI
    2. NNGroup UX Training
    3. Google UX Design Certificate (Coursera)
    4. Design Better by InVision
    5. Webflow University
    6. Learn UI Design

    Client & Business Management #

    1. Built to Sell by John Warrillow
    2. The Win Without Pitching Manifesto
    3. The Futur’s Business of Design
    4. Double Your Freelancing
    5. HaloPSA Documentation

    Strategic Planning & Process Optimization #

    1. Shape Up by Basecamp
    2. Traction by Gino Wickman
    3. Atomic Habits by James Clear
    4. Getting Things Done by David Allen

    Next Steps #

    • Prioritize learning with Refactoring UI and Shape Up.
    • Implement client strategies from The Win Without Pitching Manifesto.
    • Enhance business operations using Traction.
    • Develop a personal website with Webflow University and Figma.
