    1. PARA Method
    • Creator: Tiago Forte
    • Source: Building a Second Brain
    • Key Concepts:
      • Projects: Short-term efforts
      • Areas: Long-term responsibilities
      • Resources: Reference materials
      • Archives: Inactive items
    • Link: https://fortelabs.com/blog/para/
    1. LYT (Linking Your Thinking)
    • Creator: Nick Milo
    • Source: LYT Kit and workshops
    • Key Concepts:
      • MOCs (Maps of Content)
      • Fluid frameworks
      • Progressive summarization
    • Link: https://www.linkingyourthinking.com/
    1. Zettelkasten Method
    • Creator: Niklas Luhmann
    • Source: Original academic work and personal archive
    • Key Concepts:
      • Atomic notes
      • Unique identifiers
      • Bidirectional linking
      • Emergent structure
    • Link: https://zettelkasten.de/introduction/
    1. Johnny.Decimal System
    • Creator: Johnny Noble
    • Source: Personal organizational system
    • Key Concepts:
      • Numerical categorization (10-99)
      • Maximum 10 categories
      • Maximum 10 subcategories
      • Clear folder structure
    • Link: https://johnnydecimal.com/
    1. GTD (Getting Things Done)
    • Creator: David Allen
    • Source: Book “Getting Things Done”
    • Key Concepts:
      • Capture everything
      • Clarify the action
      • Organize by context
      • Regular reviews
    • Link: https://gettingthingsdone.com/

      • Public learning
      • Networked thought
      • Continuous growth
    • Notable Source: https://maggieappleton.com/garden-history
