    Service Level Agreement (SLA) for Web Development & Maintenance #

    1. Introduction #

    This SLA outlines the scope, response times, and billing policies for web development, UX/UI design, and website maintenance services.

    2. Scope of Services #

    • Web Development & UX/UI Design: Custom development, consulting, and optimization.
    • Website Maintenance & Support: Security updates, bug fixes, and monitoring.
    • Exclusions: Major redesigns, content creation, and extensive SEO.

    3. Service Response Times #

    Issue Type Response Time Resolution Time
    Critical 2 hours 24 hours
    High 6 hours 2-3 business
    Medium 24 hours 5 business days
    Low 48 hours As scheduled

    Urgent requests outside of these timeframes may incur an expedited service fee.

    4. Billing & Payment Terms #

    4.1 Retainer & Hourly Rates #

    • Standard Rate: $75/hour
    • Retainer Package: $1,500/month for 20 hours
    • Overage Rate: $93.75/hour for additional work beyond retainer
    • Urgent Requests (<24-hour turnaround): Billed at $93.75/hour

    4.2 Payment Schedule #

    • Retainer Clients: Invoiced monthly, payment due within 7 days.
    • Project-Based Work: 50% upfront, 50% upon completion.
    • Late Payments: A 5% late fee applies after 14 days past due.

    5. Change Requests & Scope Adjustments #

    • All scope changes must be requested in writing.
    • Small adjustments (under 1 hour) may be included within retainer.
    • Larger changes (exceeding 1 hour) will be billed separately.

    6. Termination & SLA Review #

    • Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days’ written notice.
    • SLA terms are subject to quarterly review for updates.
